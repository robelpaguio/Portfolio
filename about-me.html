About Me

Hi, I'm Robel Paguio -- "Robes" for short.

I recently left my job as a Global Learning & Development manager for WeWork in March. Now I'm a full-time student pursuing both UX/UI design through Flatiron School and front-end development through UW.

I learned how to code my first website in high school and fell in love with web design in college. It was after college when I decided to pivot, focusing on business operation and people development. While I enjoyed helping both startups and individuals grow and find success, I still carried the itch to design, so I geared up for another pivot.

I enjoy cooking as a way to distress and staying active. I've danced all my life and love ripping up the dance floor at every friend's wedding. I'm also a huge Harry Potter nerd, but I haven't tested my knowledge at a trivia night yet!
